My Favorite Games

Mobile Legends

Since 2017, I've been exploring the world of Mobile Legends. Throughout this journey with the game, I've found immense enjoyment and engaging challenges. It's not just entertainment for me; it's also an avenue to build strong relationships with friends. We often gather to play together, working as a team to improve our rankings. Each match teaches us something new and provides unforgettable moments of excitement. For us, Mobile Legends isn't just a game; it's a social experience that strengthens our friendships.

Genshin Impact

Since September 5, 2021, I've immersed myself in the world of Genshin Impact. Initially, I felt extremely confused about how to play, the game's features, and everything else within it. This confusion led to frustration, and I was on the verge of uninstalling Genshin Impact from my device. However, everything changed when I met a fellow online player who also enjoyed Genshin Impact. Patiently, they taught me every aspect of the game, from the basics to more advanced strategies. Thanks to their help and encouragement, I navigated through those challenging times and have continued to enjoy the adventures in the world of Genshin Impact to this day.
