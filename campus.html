Institute of Business Technology Sabda Setia

The Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business (ITBSS) has been operating since 2021 with a focus on advancing Indonesia onto the global stage. ITBSS offers quality education and comprehensive facilities to unleash students potential. Its main strengths lie in state-of-the-art technological infrastructure and collaborative spaces for ideas and innovation. Students are encouraged to collaborate, share knowledge, and build extensive networks. ITBSS also pays special attention to the development of moral and spiritual values through spiritual activities and character development programs. The faculty at ITBSS are experienced experts committed to providing quality education. They are not just mentors in the classroom but also partners in students journey to success.


Throughout my education at ITBSS, I have had an extraordinary experience. A positive environment, support from good friends, and the availability of responsive faculty have been valuable assets in my academic journey. Course materials are delivered effectively, catering to the needs and abilities of students, ensuring active participation from everyone. Equally important, the computer lab facilities equipped with high-performance PCs have made their usage very comfortable.
