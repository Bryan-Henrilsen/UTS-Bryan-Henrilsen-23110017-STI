My Hobbies

Playing Basketball

Since the 2nd grade of elementary school, I have been involved in the world of basketball. Initially, I joined the basketball club called "Jambore" and actively participated in the school's basketball extracurricular activities. My enthusiasm for this sport continued to grow, even when I graduated from high school. I not only regularly practiced, but also actively participated in various competitions between clubs and schools. Even at the college level, I maintained my involvement in the basketball organization. For me, basketball is not just a sport, but also an integral part of my life journey that has shaped my character and competitive spirit.

Watch Animes, Drama and Read Webtoon

To fill my leisure time, I enjoy watching anime, dramas, and reading webtoons and manga. This hobby has been part of my routine since I was in 9th grade of junior high school, and my interest in it has continued to grow until now. I find pleasure and inspiration in the stories presented in the form of animation and comics, and I enjoy the journeys offered by interesting characters and deep storylines.
