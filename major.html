Second Generation of System and Technology Department Evening Class

        In the journey through the Second Generation of System and Technology Department Evening Class, I feel a deep sense of gratitude for the opportunity to meet and get to know my fellow comrades. Their presence not only serves as a pillar during difficult times but also as an invaluable source of support in navigating the academic journey.
        On the other hand, the presence of our lecturers complements our learning experience with invaluable expertise. They not only deliver materials with exceptional professionalism but also share valuable experiences from the professional world. This not only inspires us to strive harder in pursuit of knowledge but also guides us in honing the skills required in the workforce.
        Additionally, the assignment of projects by the lecturers not only fosters innovation among students but also enriches our learning experience. Often, these projects require presentations, which provide us with a golden opportunity to enhance our public speaking skills. Thus, every step in the Second Generation of System and Technology Department Evening Class not only constitutes an educational journey but also a process of character formation and readiness to face future challenges.
